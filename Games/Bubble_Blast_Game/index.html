Instructions

  • Aim and Shoot: Use the mouse to aim the bubble shooter. Adjust the angle and trajectory to your desired position. Click or tap to shoot the bubble towards the top of the grid.
  • Match Bubbles: Match three or more bubbles of the same color to make them pop and disappear.
  • Clear the Grid: Your goal is to clear the entire grid of bubbles.
  • Score Points: Earn points for each bubble you pop and for clearing the grid.
  • Game Over: The game is over if the bubbles reach the bottom of the grid before you clear them all.
