# Instructions

This game is about building a sustainable city

  1. Filling all the boxes with increasing money
    and decreasing pollution and happiness > 100 is a Win
  2. Filling all boxes with happiness less than 100 and
    money decreasing over time and pollution increasing in city is loss
  3. A factory
    • Creating costs = 200
    • generates = 50 unit money per 20sec
    • decreases happiness by 20 units
    • generates 15 unit pollution per 20sec
  4. A house
    • Creating costs = 100
    • maintainance = 20 unit money per 20sec
    • Increases happiness by 10 units
    • Generates 1 unit pollution per 20sec
  5. A park
    • Creating costs = 120
    • maintainance = 15 unit money per 20sec
    • Increases happiness by 20 units
    • Decreases pollution by 13 unit per 20sec

How to play

  1. Select the building type
  2. Fill as many boxes as you like with that building (unchangeable)
  3. Creating a building will cost money and also impact happiness and pollution

Money: 1000

Happiness: 100

Pollution: 0

You Won the Game !
