# **Infinite_Cars** 

---

<br>

## **Description 📃**
- A car racing game with infinite number of cars trying to hit players car 
- Navigate the road and its dangers while gaining maximum points.

## **How to play? 🕹️**

- Controls
    - right click and hold the car and move using mouse.
