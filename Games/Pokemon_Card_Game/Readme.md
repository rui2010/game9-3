# **Pokemon Card Game** 

---

<br>

## **Description 📃**
The goal of the Pokémon trading card game is to assemble the strongest deck of Pokémon and compete by taking turns utilizing attacks and abilities to win matches. According to the official Pokémon website, each player uses a deck of 60 cards while playing two players at once.
 It's using HTML, CSS, JavaScript  Library.

## **How to play? 🕹️**
<h3>Turn the Pokémon cards that are currently in play and those that are on the bench over to begin. Each player's turn consists of three steps:</h3>

<ol>
<li>Draw</li>
<li>Action</li>
<li>Attack</li>
</ol>

<h3>Draw a card from your deck first. You can then choose the course of action you want to pursue, such as:</h3>

Play a basic Pokémon
Evolve a Pokémon
Attach an energy card
Play a trainer card
Retreat your active Pokémon
Use your Pokémon's abilities

<h3>How to obtain reward cards</h3>
Six reward cards are placed aside by each player and are given to the rival once a round is won. The player often receives one reward card from their opponent's deck for each round they win.

A player could receive more or fewer reward cards than one, depending on the card. The winner of the game is the one who collects all of their opponent's prize cards.
